Quick summary

Narakeet is an online service that helps people make narrated videos easily. It was created for a global audience and supports 90 languages and counting. In brief, it uses artificial intelligence to create life-like narration from speaker notes in a presentation. Users can edit video as simply as they would edit text, saving hours by not having to record and re-record audio, synchronise picture with sound and transcribe subtitles.
